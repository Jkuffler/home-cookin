French Toast

Ingredients

  • 5-6 slices sourdough: ideally set out to dry for a few hours
  • 1.5 cup creamy oat milk: room temperature
  • 2 eggs: room temperature
  • ground cinnamon
  • vanilla extract
  • desired fruits
  • half stick of preferred butter: room temp
  • syrup or honey
  • optional: oatmeal
  • powdered sugar
  • cinnamon sugar

Steps

  1. preheat skillet to medium
  2. for juicier fruit: wash & cut fruit then heat at 400-450 F in the oven while cooking french toast
  3. add vanilla to milk: to taste or color preference
  4. carefully whisk in one egg at a time into milk
  5. whisk thoroughly egg whites and yolk: looking for a smooth, thick, wet and creamy batter (small air bubbles are fine as long as your aren't heating the batter by over-whisking)
  6. sprinkle desired cinnamon onto batter - mix if you like or let it sit atop batter
  7. at the skillet: raise heat slightly and apply a chip of butter per piece of bread
  8. gently place bread onto batter, flip
  9. if needed: gently remove excess batter back to container
  10. for evenly cooked french toast: cook each side until brown - adding light butter when flipping to uncooked side
  11. optional: sprinkle a layer of oatmeal onto wet french toast and cook into bread for an extra crunch
  12. turn off skillet or pan
  13. save extra batter for no more than 24hrs
  14. add fruit - fresh or baked
  15. add maple syrup or honey to taste
  16. sprinkle cinnamon sugar and/or powdered sugar - serve and enjoy
