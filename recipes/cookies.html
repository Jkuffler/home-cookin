Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Planning to share? These freshly baked, from-scratch cookies will make you super popular!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 sticks room-temp unsalted butter
  • 1.25 cups granular sugar
  • .75 cups brown sugar
  • 2 room-temp eggs
  • (Premium) Vanilla Extract - to taste
  • 2.25 cups flour
  • 1-1.5 tsp Baking Soda
  • Pink Himalayan Sea Salt - to taste
  • 2 cups preferred Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. preheat conventional oven to 350 degrees F (adjust temp for high altitudes)
  2. lightly mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar to a sugary paste
  3. lightly mix in eggs separately - one at a time
  4. slowly mix vanilla extract to desired color/flavor
  5. use a separate bowl for dry ingredients starting with flour
  6. add baking soda
  7. add sea salt
  8. gently whisk together to ensure air is not caught inside dry mix
  9. once dry mix is stirred well: slowly mix dry into wet ingredients
  10. gently blend in chocolate chips
  11. bake ~6 minutes and rotate tray
  12. bake additional ~5 minutes or until cookies have spread and have desired color
  13. repeat until batter is gone or store the batter for a later
  14. turn off oven
  15. cool cookies for a few hours and enjoy
  16. store extra cookies in air-tight container to share with neighbors, friends, coworkers

Consuming raw or uncooked ingredients/mixes could be harmful to one's health
